# Consolidation Guide

Use this guide after research agents finish their work.

## Consolidation Order

1. Review each agent's `handoff.md`.
2. Merge source metadata into `research/00_source_index.md`.
3. Merge evidence items by claim category and confidence.
4. Resolve conflicting claims by source quality, geography, recency, and direct relevance to German quote-stage behavior.
5. Fill the final deliverables from `research/01_reonic_public_ecosystem.md` through `research/16_open_questions.md`.
6. Create the final synthesis in `RESEARCH_SUMMARY.md`.

## Required Checks

- Every final claim has at least one source.
- Germany-specific claims are separated from transferable non-Germany claims.
- Vendor claims are labeled.
- Weak claims are not promoted to product rules.
- Each persona, objection, channel tactic, and action recommendation maps to observable product data or a missing-data prompt.
- The summary includes product implications, not just research facts.

## Summary Standard

`RESEARCH_SUMMARY.md` should be detailed enough for builders to implement the PoC without re-reading every raw source, but it must preserve citations and confidence levels for important claims.
