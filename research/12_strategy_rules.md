## Claim Safety Rules

- Claims about tariffs, feed-in rates, tax, finance, subsidies, local rules, installation timelines, savings, payback, battery ROI, heat-pump savings, and urgency require a source and checked date.
- If the source is missing or stale, generate an internal task rather than homeowner-facing copy.
- If a claim cannot be made safely, explain the missing data to the installer.

## Scoring Rules

- Use rule-based scores for the PoC.
- Show factor chips, not opaque model output.
- Let installer override the recommendation with a required reason.
- Treat ghosting and close readiness as hypotheses until validated with signed/lost outcomes.
