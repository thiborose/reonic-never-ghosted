# Evidence Item Template

Use one record per extracted claim or insight.

```yaml
evidence_id:
claim:
claim_category:
supporting_source_ids:
evidence_type:
strength_of_evidence: unknown
geography:
region_applicability:
persona_applicability:
funnel_stage:
quote_stage_relevance:
quote_or_data_excerpt:
counterevidence:
limitations:
product_implication:
message_implication:
action_implication:
data_fields_required:
validation_needed:
```

Use `action_implication` for calls, visits, calendar tasks, proposal revisions, pauses, and debrief needs.
