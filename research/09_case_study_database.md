## Case Study Implications

- The PoC should not use public customer names as homeowner-facing proof without permission.
- Reonic case signals justify two installer modes: constrained owner-led installer and growing process-heavy installer.
- Competitive signals show that CRM, design, quotation, ERP, portal, and operations are crowded. The stronger PoC angle is post-quote decision orchestration: blocker diagnosis, proof, consent, scheduling, and debrief.
- Vendor metrics can inspire hypotheses but should not be used as proof of Reonic assistant impact.
