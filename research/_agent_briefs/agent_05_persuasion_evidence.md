# Agent 5 Brief: Persuasion Evidence

## Mission

Identify proven, ethical sales and marketing methods relevant to high-consideration residential renewable energy decisions.

## Work Folder

Use only: `research/agents/agent_05_persuasion_evidence/`

## Core Questions

- Which tactics are supported for trust, risk reduction, follow-up timing, social proof, commitment, loss aversion, objection handling, and decision-friction reduction?
- Which tactics are validated in solar, home improvement, high-consideration B2C sales, or only general marketing?
- Which tactics are risky, manipulative, or non-compliant?
- How should the assistant explain tactics to installers without exposing crude persuasion logic to homeowners?
- Which tactics are testable in future A/B experiments?

## Required Outputs

- Source records in `sources.md`.
- Evidence items in `evidence.md`.
- Findings in `findings.md`.
- Handoff in `handoff.md`.
- Open questions in `open_questions.md`.

## Research Rules

- Prefer field evidence and credible studies over generic marketing blogs.
- Label indirect evidence clearly.
- Define ethical red lines.
