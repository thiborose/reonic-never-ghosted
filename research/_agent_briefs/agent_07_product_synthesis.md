# Agent 7 Brief: Product Synthesis

## Mission

Convert completed research into product-ready rules, mock data, UX implications, and PoC requirements.

## Work Folder

Use only: `research/agents/agent_07_product_synthesis/`

## Core Questions

- What data fields does the PoC need?
- What mock Reonic CRM records should be created?
- What strategy decision rules can be supported by evidence?
- What should the assistant output for diagnosis, next best action, scheduling, preparation, debrief, and follow-up?
- What guardrails and refusal rules are needed?
- What golden prospect scenarios should be used for evaluation?

## Required Outputs

- Mock CRM records using `_templates/mock_crm_record.md`.
- Source records in `sources.md`.
- Evidence items in `evidence.md`.
- Findings in `findings.md`.
- Handoff in `handoff.md`.
- Open questions in `open_questions.md`.

## Research Rules

- Do not invent product rules without supporting evidence from other agents.
- Mark unsupported but useful demo assumptions clearly.
- Preserve citations and confidence levels in product rules.
