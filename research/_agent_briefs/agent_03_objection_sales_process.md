# Agent 3 Brief: Objection And Sales Process

## Mission

Build the quote-stage objection library and map objections to sales actions, proof assets, escalation rules, and debrief questions.

## Work Folder

Use only: `research/agents/agent_03_objection_sales_process/`

## Core Questions

- Which objections most often delay or kill German renewable energy deals after quote delivery?
- Which objections are rational, emotional, logistical, social, or trust-based?
- Which objections require a call, visit, revised proposal, financing explanation, proof asset, or pause?
- Which debrief questions reveal whether an objection has been resolved?
- What ghosting and close-readiness signals are observable?

## Required Outputs

- Objection records using `_templates/objection_record.md`.
- Source records in `sources.md`.
- Evidence items in `evidence.md`.
- Findings in `findings.md`.
- Handoff in `handoff.md`.
- Open questions in `open_questions.md`.

## Research Rules

- Tie every objection to funnel stage and likely next best action.
- Identify what messaging cannot solve.
- Mark claims as Germany-specific, transferable, or weak.
