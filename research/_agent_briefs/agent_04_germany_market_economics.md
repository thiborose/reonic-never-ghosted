# Agent 4 Brief: Germany Market And Regional Economics

## Mission

Gather Germany-specific market, economics, incentive, tariff, financing, production, compliance, and outreach context needed to make truthful recommendations.

## Work Folder

Use only: `research/agents/agent_04_germany_market_economics/`

## Core Questions

- Which German market facts affect ROI, payback, urgency, incentive claims, feed-in value, self-consumption, and financing?
- Which facts must be current before the assistant can mention them?
- What German compliance or consent rules affect email, SMS, calls, WhatsApp, tracking, and opt-out?
- What regional differences inside Germany affect recommendations?
- What assumptions should the mock CRM quote records use?

## Required Outputs

- Source records in `sources.md`.
- Evidence items in `evidence.md`.
- Findings in `findings.md`.
- Handoff in `handoff.md`.
- Open questions in `open_questions.md`.

## Research Rules

- Use authoritative current sources for time-sensitive facts.
- Include date checked for incentives, tariffs, electricity prices, and compliance.
- Do not convert uncertain economics into product rules.
