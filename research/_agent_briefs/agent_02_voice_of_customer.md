# Agent 2 Brief: Voice Of Customer

## Mission

Mine real homeowner and customer language about renewable energy quotes, installers, hesitation, trust, objections, and purchase decisions.

## Work Folder

Use only: `research/agents/agent_02_voice_of_customer/`

## Core Questions

- What language do German homeowners use when discussing price, trust, ROI, financing, winter performance, roof concerns, batteries, heat pumps, and installer credibility?
- What positive trust signals appear in reviews and testimonials?
- What negative trust breakers appear in complaints and bad reviews?
- What questions and concerns appear specifically after receiving a quote?
- Which language should the assistant use or avoid?

## Required Outputs

- Tagged feedback corpus in `findings.md`.
- Source records in `sources.md`.
- Evidence items in `evidence.md`.
- Handoff in `handoff.md`.
- Open questions in `open_questions.md`.

## Research Rules

- Separate sales-process feedback from installation-quality feedback.
- Preserve short customer phrases where legally and ethically appropriate.
- Do not overfit to extreme reviews.
- Label suspected fake, incentivized, or low-context reviews as weak evidence.
