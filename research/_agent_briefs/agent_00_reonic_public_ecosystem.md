# Agent 0 Brief: Reonic Public Ecosystem And Product Scope

## Mission

Research Reonic's public ecosystem to determine the most credible product and installer scope for the PoC.

## Work Folder

Use only: `research/agents/agent_00_reonic_public_ecosystem/`

## Core Questions

- What products and workflows does Reonic publicly emphasize for installers in Germany?
- Which public customer stories, logos, testimonials, partners, or references can be identified?
- What do identifiable Reonic-connected installers offer on their own websites?
- What technology mix should the PoC prioritize: PV, battery, heat pump, EV charger, or multi-technology household energy?
- What Reonic-like CRM, quote, proposal, calendar, communication, and workflow data should be mocked?

## Required Outputs

- Source records in `sources.md`.
- Evidence items in `evidence.md`.
- Findings in `findings.md`.
- Handoff in `handoff.md`.
- Open questions in `open_questions.md`.

## Research Rules

- Prefer Germany-specific evidence.
- Label public-logo or testimonial evidence as public signal, not verified current usage.
- Clearly separate facts from inference.
- Do not use private data or contact companies.
