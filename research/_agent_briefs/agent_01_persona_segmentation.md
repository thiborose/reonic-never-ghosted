# Agent 1 Brief: Persona And Segmentation

## Mission

Validate and expand homeowner personas for German post-quote renewable energy sales.

## Work Folder

Use only: `research/agents/agent_01_persona_segmentation/`

## Core Questions

- Which homeowner personas appear in German solar and renewable energy buying evidence?
- Which personas from non-Germany sources are likely transferable?
- What observable signals could a Reonic-like assistant use to infer each persona?
- Which personas differ meaningfully in objections, proof needs, channel preference, timing, tone, and next best action?

## Required Outputs

- Persona cards using `_templates/persona_card.md`.
- Source records in `sources.md`.
- Evidence items in `evidence.md`.
- Findings in `findings.md`.
- Handoff in `handoff.md`.
- Open questions in `open_questions.md`.

## Research Rules

- Germany evidence first.
- Avoid stereotypes that cannot be inferred from available product data.
- Mark mixed-persona cases explicitly.
- Include what messages or actions backfire for each persona.
